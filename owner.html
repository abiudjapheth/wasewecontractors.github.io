About Abiud

Abiud Wasewe Maombo is the Founder of Wasewe Contractors, a forward-thinking construction and design firm dedicated to delivering practical, innovative, and cost-effective solutions across Kenya. He integrates architectural creativity with strong financial expertise to ensure that every project is not only structurally sound but also economically efficient and sustainably executed.

Education

He is currently pursuing a Diploma in Architectural Technology at The Eldoret National Polytechnic, alongside the Accounting Technician Diploma (ATD) with KASNEB through RCM Online College. This multidisciplinary academic path equips him with a solid foundation in design principles, construction methodology, and financial management, enabling a well-balanced approach to the built environment.

Professional Experience

His expertise also extends to cost estimation, project coordination, and multidisciplinary collaboration. He is recognized for his attention to detail, strong work ethic, and unwavering commitment to high-quality outcomes.

Vision & Mission

Through Wasewe Contractors, Abiud envisions building a reputable and trusted brand that delivers reliable architectural, construction, and project management services across Kenya—anchored on professionalism, innovation, and client satisfaction.
